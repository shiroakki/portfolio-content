A little about me

Hi, there! I'm Renan Chen, currently a fresh-year graduate student in the Master of Science in
Information program at University of Michigan. I had my bachelor's degree in electrical and computer
engineering, and now I'm beginning a new life chapter in information studies and design! My primary
interests include UX/UI design, graphic design and interaction design.

This is the official homepage of mine. You can explore more about me on the navigation bar (to be
implemented). Besides professional interests, I'm also passionate about life. I love iced coffee and
film art. I was in an A cappella chorus group for three years and performed on stages quite often.
I also do photography in my spare time. For those details you could check the gallery page!>>>
