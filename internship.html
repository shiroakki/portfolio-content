Ruijin-miHoYo Collaborative Neuroscience Lab.

My primary role at RMLab was as a research intern. The goal is to find innovative, machine learning-supported

identifiers such as heart rate variability and sleep patterns to predict treatment-resistant depression from
wearable devices using machine learning algorithms.

I worked in Coca Cola CPS as a strategic sourcing intern for six months. I helped make strategic decisions

sustainability practices. The main tool I used was Python and MS Excel.
